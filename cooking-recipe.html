Best chocolate chip cookies

This recipe is from my dad and they ars favorite among friends and family. The secret incredient is coconut! be warned, this will fly of the plate.

ingredients

  • butter
  • white sugar
  • brown sugar
  • eggs
  • vanilla
  • flour
  • baking soda
  • salt
  • chocolate chip
  • oatmeal
  • coconut

directions

  1. preheat the oven to 325
  2. beat the butter, eggs, sugar and vanilla together until creamy
  3. mix together the flour, baking soda and salt in separate bowl
  4. add flour mixture to butter mixture slowly
  5. stir in chocolate chips, oatmeal and coconut
  6. bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown.
